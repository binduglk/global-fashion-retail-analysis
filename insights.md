# Key Insights — Global Fashion Retail Analysis

- **Geographic concentration risk:** China contributes ~73-74% of total revenue, far ahead of any other market (US, Germany, France, Portugal, Spain, UK combined make up the rest). This signals heavy dependency on a single market — diversifying into other regions would reduce this risk.

- **Store performance:** Shanghai is the top-performing store by a significant margin, followed by other Chinese stores, then US and European locations. Worth investigating what Shanghai's store does differently (staffing, local demand, marketing) to replicate in underperforming locations.

- **Category performance:** Feminine and Masculine clothing categories drive the overwhelming majority of revenue, while Children's category is comparatively small — could indicate an opportunity to expand the children's line, or confirm it's intentionally a smaller focus area.

- **Employee performance:** A small group of sales associates consistently outperform peers — worth studying their approach for training/onboarding other staff.

- **Data validation:** Cross-checked key metrics (revenue by store, category, and country) in both Power BI and SQL — results matched consistently across both tools, confirming the analysis is accurate and not tool-dependent.
